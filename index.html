Hey There! Welcome to the orizer demo page.

This is a jQuery plugin to make funny things happen on the internet. It expands the baddass raptorize plugin by zurb and lets you do other orizing to webpages. Out of the box this yayQuery plugin gives you the ability to Hodorize, Trogdorize, and Nick Cagorize the page. If that's not enough you also get a "your own custom thingorize" to slap in your own jokes. yay jokes.

Raptorize me to activate the orize plugin in raptor mode.

Hodorize me to activate the orize plugin in hodor mode.

Cagorize me to activate the orize plugin in cage mode.

Trogdorize me to activate the orize plugin in trogdor mode.

You can also use the Konami-Code
(on your keyboard type ↑ ↑ ↓ ↓ ← → ← → B A)

You can also wait around for a specific amount of time from pageload. (5000 ms)
